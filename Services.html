Our Services

CT scan Service

CT scans are essential for diagnosing and monitoring various diseases and
conditions, including cancer, heart disease, lung disorders, and neurological
conditions like strokes and brain tumors. They can reveal the size, location,
and characteristics of abnormalities within the body.

Eye Examination

These appointments, typically conducted by optometrists or ophthalmologists,
involve a series of tests and examinations to evaluate various aspects of your
vision and detect any potential eye problems.

Emergency

The primary goal of ER services is to stabilize patients and provide timely medical
care to address their urgent needs. Upon arrival, patients are typically triaged based
on the severity of their condition, with those requiring immediate attention seen first.
This prioritization ensures that critical cases receive prompt medical intervention.

Ultrasound

Ultrasound, also known as ultrasonography, is a non-invasive medical imaging technique
that uses high-frequency sound waves to create real-time images of the inside of the
body.Ultrasound imaging can be used to evaluate various parts of the body, including the
abdomen, pelvis, heart, blood vessels, and musculoskeletal system.
